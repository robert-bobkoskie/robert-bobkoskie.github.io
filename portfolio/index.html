ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

A study of  the concepts, methods, and prominent issues in Knowledge-Based Artificial Intelligence and the application of these concepts to the design of knowledge-based AI agents. KBAI elucidate the relationship between knowledge-based artificial intelligence and the study of human cognition. I developed an AI Agent to solve Raven's Progressive Matrices (RPM). RPM is a nonverbal test that measures abstract reasoning and provides an estimate of fluid intelligence. Sample problem: Raven's Progressive Matrices (RPM)

MACHINE LEARNING

This project explores Reinforcement Learning (RL) through the evaluation of both two, and three-dimensional Grid-World MDPs using Markov Decision Processes (MDP), Policy/Value Iteration and Q-Learning, MDP problem-solving methods utilized the parameters: {Discount, Noise, Living Reward, for Policy/Value Iteration}, and {α: Learning Rate, ε: Action Selection for Q-Learning}. For reference, a Grid-World is defined as a deterministic game using Policy/Value Iteration to evaluate Q-Learning/Reinforcement Learning. The grid is typically a two-dimensional {x, y} space comprised of cells hosting: values for reward, barriers, and start/end locations. I chose to develop a three-dimensional grid with {x, y, z} to compare a more complex learning task vs. the two-dimensional space.

  • REQUIREMENTS: Reinforcement Learning
  • CODE, DEVELOPMENT: Reinforcement Learning
  • RESULTS DOCUMENTATION: Reinforcement Learning
  • TRAINING, OPERATION, MAINTENANCE: Reinforcement Learning
