ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

A study of  the concepts, methods, and prominent issues in Knowledge-Based Artificial Intelligence and the application of these concepts to the design of knowledge-based AI agents. KBAI elucidate the relationship between knowledge-based artificial intelligence and the study of human cognition. I developed an AI Agent to solve Raven's Progressive Matrices (RPM). RPM is a nonverbal test that measures abstract reasoning and provides an estimate of fluid intelligence. Sample problem: Raven's Progressive Matrices (RPM)

  • CODE, DEVELOPMENT: Artificial Intelligence
  • RESULTS DOCUMENTATION: Artificial Intelligence
  • TRAINING, OPERATION, MAINTENANCE: Artificial Intelligence
